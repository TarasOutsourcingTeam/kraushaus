The Conversation

You tell us about your property and your vision — a rough idea, a sketch, or a full plan. We listen, ask the right questions, and tell you honestly what's possible, what it costs, and how long it takes. No sales pitch. Just facts.

02

Land & Location

If you're still looking for the right property, we can help you evaluate plots — terrain, access, soil, local restrictions. We know what makes a Tuscan site buildable and what makes it a trap.

03

Permits & Italian Compliance

We coordinate the full permit process — Comune, Genio Civile, structural clearances, safety documentation. Our local technical partners handle Italian bureaucracy in Italian. You handle none of it.

04

Design & Budget

We work with your architect or introduce you to ours. Once the design is set, we prepare a full itemized budget — transparent, with a clear structure and no hidden costs. You know what is fixed, what is estimated, and where restoration-specific contingencies may apply before work begins.

Construction

Work begins. Every week — photos, videos, progress updates to your phone. Every key stage — a technical inspection by our engineers. You always know what's happening. You never have to wonder.

Honest Answers

Your Questions.
Our Honest Answers.

We don't have a property yet. Can we still talk?

Absolutely. The earlier, the better. We help you understand what's realistic, what regions make sense for your goals, and what the full process looks like from the very beginning.

How does your pricing work? Will costs change?

Reconstruction in Tuscany involves real complexity — soil conditions, historic structures, permit requirements. We don't make promises we can't keep. What we do: a detailed itemized budget before work starts, full transparency at every stage, and no surprise invoices. Every cost is explained before it's approved.

How do you manage quality when we're not on-site?

Weekly video and photo reports. A dedicated project manager as your single point of contact. Stage-by-stage technical inspections. Full visibility at all times — from anywhere in the world.

We don't speak Italian. Is that a problem?

Not for a single day. We handle all communication with local authorities, suppliers and subcontractors. You work with us — in English or German.

How long does it take?

A full renovation: 10–14 months. New construction from foundation: 14–20 months. We give you a realistic timeline from day one — not an optimistic one designed to make you sign.

What if something doesn't meet the standard?

We fix it. At our cost. That's not a policy — that's the only way we know how to work. Full liability is covered in the contract.
